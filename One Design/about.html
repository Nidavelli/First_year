Our Story

A Passion for Pizza

Unexpected Inspiration
It all started with a burnt pizza. Yes, you read that right! While attempting to recreate a gourmet pizza at home, we ended up with a slightly charred masterpiece. The smokey flavor paired surprisingly well with the tangy tomato sauce and gooey cheese. This "happy accident" sparked our passion for experimenting with unique flavor combinations. At Pizza Pie, we don't shy away from the unexpected. We use fresh, seasonal ingredients and innovative toppings to create pizzas that are both familiar and exciting.

We weren't born overnight pizza masters! While we had Nonna's legendary recipe as a foundation, recreating her magic was no easy feat. We spent countless hours perfecting the dough - mastering the balance of chew and crisp, and achieving that signature rise. We experimented with different flours, hydration levels, and fermentation techniques until we finally captured Nonna's signature lightness. But the journey didn't stop there. We sourced the freshest ingredients, from vine-ripened tomatoes to creamy mozzarella made locally. We even built a custom brick oven to replicate the intense heat that creates that perfect char on the crust. It wasn't always smooth sailing - there were burnt pizzas, soggy bottoms, and frustratingly flat doughs. But with each challenge, we learned and adapted, fueled by our passion for pizza and the desire to share Nonna's legacy.
